“...One purple two-headed figure emerged from the inside of the spaceship. Its two heads and 6 six pairs of eyes started to scan the environment in disbelief. One of the heads then uttered some words in an alien-ish language that would probably translate in human tongue to something like “Damn those humans have done it again”..."

“...The second head replied: “This is the 4th time they’ve destroyed their planet”. “Time for a reset”. “Yeppp..” the first head agreed. The two-headed figure returned to the ship..."
